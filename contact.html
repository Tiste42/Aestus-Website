Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Aestus Consulting for?

We specialize in biotech and pharma companies looking to transform their AI capabilities. Whether you're a startup or an established enterprise, if you're ready to move beyond surface-level AI use, we can help.

What makes your approach different?

We focus on culture change, not just tool training. We believe the AI models are already good enough. What's missing is the human layer. Our "Give, Teach, Build" methodology delivers immediate value while building lasting capability.

How long does a typical engagement last?

It depends on your goals. A CEO "Art of the Possible" session is 90 minutes. Team training programs typically span several weeks. Ongoing partnerships can last months or years as we help you scale.

Do you work with companies that already have AI tools?

Absolutely. Many of our clients have Copilot, ChatGPT Enterprise, or other tools but aren't getting the value they expected. We help you maximize your existing investments before recommending new ones.

What's the first step?

Take our AI Quick Check survey above, or email us directly. We'll schedule a brief call to understand your situation and recommend the best path forward.

Is my data safe with you?

Yes. We take data security seriously, especially in regulated industries like biotech and pharma. We can work within your existing security frameworks and never require access to sensitive data to deliver value.
