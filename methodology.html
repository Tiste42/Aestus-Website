Give → Teach → Build

Our three-phase approach ensures immediate value while building lasting capability.

1

Give the Fish

(Wow Moments)

Goal: Eliminate skepticism. Create the "a-ha" moment. Secure buy-in.

What We Do: High-impact demos using your own company data. Show what's possible before asking anyone to do anything.

Deliverables:

  • Live demonstrations (Personal Profiler, Company Profiler)
  • Proof that AI understands your business
  • Excitement, not homework

Typical: CEO "Art of the Possible" Session (90 minutes)

2

Teach to Fish

(Mastery Training)

Goal: Transform surface-level users into confident AI practitioners.

What We Do: Train them to do it themselves. Focus on frameworks, not just tools.

Core Content:

  • The Simple Pattern (Context Engineering)
  • The Iteration Imperative
  • The Intern Analogy
  • Use Case Prioritization

Typical: Follow-up sessions, practice coaching, team workshops

3

Build Safe Harbor

(Scale & Sustain)

Goal: Transform individual wins into organizational capability.

What We Do: Help build systems that last... shared resources, governance, custom solutions.

Deliverables (Progressive):

  • Internal prompt library / knowledge hub
  • Governance guidelines
  • Custom agents for specific workflows
  • (Later) Advanced integrations

Typical: Ongoing relationship, department rollouts

The Client Journey

From skeptic to AI-first: a realistic progression through six stages of transformation.

Click image to enlarge

Stage 0

Pre-Awareness

Profile: Haven't really tried AI, or tried once and gave up

Mindset: Skeptical, overwhelmed, or dismissive

Need: A "wow" moment proving relevance to their work

Stage 1

Curious & Practicing

Profile: Sees value, starting to use AI personally

Mindset: "This could work for me"

Need: Frameworks, practice, confidence

Stage 2

Building Confidence

Profile: Using AI regularly, seeing results

Mindset: "How do I get better? How do I share this?"

Need: Advanced techniques, peer learning

Stage 3

Spreading to Team

Profile: Leader wants to scale wins across department

Mindset: "How do we get everyone doing this?"

Need: Role-specific training, shared resources

Stage 4

Systematizing

Profile: AI becoming part of how the org works

Mindset: "How do we make this sustainable?"

Need: Governance frameworks, custom solutions

Stage 5

Advanced Integration

Profile: AI embedded, ready for next level

Mindset: "What else is possible?"

Need: Technical integrations, automation at scale
