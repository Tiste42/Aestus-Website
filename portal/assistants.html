What is an Assistant?

An assistant is an AI configured with a specific "system prompt" that shapes its behavior for a particular task. Instead of explaining what you need every time, you set it up once and reuse it.

Think of it like training a new team member once, then just handing them work. The system prompt tells the AI: "You are an expert at X. When given Y, you do Z. Always follow these rules..."
