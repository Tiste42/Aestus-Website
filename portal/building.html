Building with AI

Creating your own tools, visuals, and assets without a development team.

You Are a Builder Now

You don't need to know Python or Javascript. If you can speak your native language, you can code. We call this "Vibe Coding" or "Create with Code."

You can spin up simple apps to solve your own problems—a gym tracker, a daily planner, a motivation app, or a simple game from your childhood—just by describing what you want.

The Builder's Toolkit

NotebookLM (Google)

Superpower: Instant expertise & Audio Overviews.

Upload 50 PDFs, websites, or slides. It grounds its answers only in those sources (no hallucinations). It can also generate a "Deep Dive" audio podcast where two AI hosts discuss your material. Incredible for learning while commuting.

Nano Banana Pro (Google)

Superpower: Text-Perfect Visuals & Context.

Powered by Gemini 3. It nails text in images (no gibberish) and compresses large ideas into stunning visual representations. It understands context deeply, making it the ultimate tool for turning abstract strategies into concrete visuals.

Cursor / Replit

Superpower: Building Software.

Use "Composer" mode to write natural language instructions ("Make a website that tracks my water intake") and watch it write the code, fix errors, and deploy it.

Multimodal Tools

Superpower: Content Expansion.

Turn a blog post into a video script. Turn that script into a voiceover with ElevenLabs. Turn that voiceover into a video with HeyGen or Sora. One idea, many formats.

Start Small, Have Fun

Don't try to build the next Facebook. Build a "Daily Affirmation Generator" for yourself. Play is the fastest way to learn these tools.
